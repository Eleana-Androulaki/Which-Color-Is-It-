How To Play ×

  • Select "New Game" to begin a new game

  • Game Rules

    The goal of each level is to select the right color based on the color description.

    On the left side of the screen, the three basic color components appear along with the percentage that each contributes to the current level's goal color

    The game is simple. Click on the color on the right that matches exactly the color produced by the percentages on the left.

    • Above the color section, you can find the game stats. Those stats consist of the following:

    1. Lives: The mistaken choices you have left.
    2. Score: The current game's score.
    3. Level: The current level.
    4. Highscore: Your highscore.

  • Up to level 60

    Each time you click on a wrong color, this color disappears and a you lose a life and your score is diminished.

    Each time you click on a correct color, you proceed to the next level. You also gain a gradually augmenting amount on your score.

    For every 5 levels that you pass, you gain one life back.

    If you lose all your lives, then you return to the last checkpoint. The checkpoints are on levels 1, 16, 31 and 46. Your score is diminished and you can start again from your checkpoint level having a specific amount of lives that differs for each checkpoint.

    Between two consecutive checkpoints, the number of possible colors to choose remains constant. However, once you pass from one checkpoint into another, three more colors appear. In short:

    1. Levels 1-15: 3 colors.
    2. Levels 16-30: 6 colors.
    3. Levels 31-45: 9 colors.
    4. Levels 46-60: 12 colors.

  • Levels 61-65

    After level 60 the game rules change significantly. Instead of the percentage of Red, Yellow and Blue components, you now have to pick the right color based on its RGB (Red, Green, Blue) VALUE and not percentage. Each RGB component has a value that varies between 0 and 255. That means that, for example the color RGB(41,37,129) consists of Red : 41/255 , Green : 37/255 and Blue : 129/255.

    For each correct answer on these levels, you gain back 1 life. Also each level adds 3 colors. The final level, 65, has 12 colors.

    If you lose all your lives within these five final levels, you return to the level 46 checkpoint. This means that the final checkpoint consists of 20 levels rather than 15.
